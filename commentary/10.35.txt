I am tatha, also; the Brhat-sama, the foremost samnam, of the Sama-mantras. Chandasam, of the metres, of the Rk-mantras having the metres Gayatri etc.; I am the Rk called Gayatri. This ismeaning. Masanam, of the months, I am Marga sirsa (Agrahayana, November-December). Rtunam, of the seasons; kusumakarah, spring.
